Interface System

Interface System Prototype

For this first system prototype, I wanted to take the sketchbook home page and simplify the already existing information. This included making the information more clear with headers. One of the main reasons for this was how the information was getting lost on a cellphone display. Because of the format some of the links were becoming hard to click with a finger on the phone. Making the links bigger with a clear separation allows for easier navigation for phone users and emphasizes visibility. Another change was clearly separating the information into their categories. Some of the weeks had demo links in them even though there was a separate section for that. I guess this could be considered a step in preventing errors and/or ensuring there is a consistency through the entire system. The previous version made navigation a little challenging as it seemed overall unorganized. One change that I would like to implement is an even clearer separation of the information. I am thinking about removing the ‘Week #’ category entirely and just separating the links based on Sketches, Demos, Microproject, and Readings. If I were to do this, I would have to move where the dates are displayed so that there is a clear timeline. Possibly having the dates show up when hovering on the link may make the landing page less cluttered overall. Another option I was looking into was having a dropdown menu instead of displaying all the links at once. I believe adding a dropdown might make navigation feel a little more intuitive to those that are familiar with sites like Amazon or Apple. I believe adding the dropdown adds a layer of feedback to the user, conveying that their input was received. The hope is to make the system simple so that navigating the homepage is satisfying to the user.
