Ideas

Dont Press

This interaction conveys the feeling of temptation because being 'too anxious' forces the user to wait a little longer.

The sketch has a timer that slowly counts down in a set time. If the area is pressed 'or maybe checks movement' it adds time to the timer.

waiting, relief, calm, anxiety

Burnout

Over time this interaction imitates the feeling of burnout with the slowing of the sketch

The drawing copies the movement of the mouse, but it slowly gets lagged behing. To catch back up to speed, the user needs to move the mouse faster to get more energy.

tired, slow, heavy, annoying

Work Hard

Some people get to make alot of money, others have to work hard to make enough

The interaction has two counters on both sides. Every mouse click adds the US minimum wage to one of the counters. The other interaction could either be an exponential growth or how much money Amazon makes in a second. The competition is supposed to be impossible for the user to beat.

Annoying, sad, waiting
